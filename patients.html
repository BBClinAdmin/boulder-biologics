From anywhere

Telemedicine consultations.

Consult with us remotely.

Boulder Biologics offers orthobiologic consultations by video for private insurance carriers that cover remote medicine. We see patients from across the country, and a remote consultation is an easy first step before traveling to the clinic.

To arrange a telemedicine visit, call 720-550-6175 or email info@boulderbiologics.com and our team will coordinate scheduling and confirm coverage.
