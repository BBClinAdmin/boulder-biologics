What it is

What longevity medicine actually is.

Longevity medicine asks a different question than an annual physical. A physical screens for current disease. A longevity assessment quantifies your biological age and trajectory, across hormones, metabolism, cognition, mitochondrial function, and the underlying epigenetic clock, and then builds a personalized plan to modify that trajectory before disease emerges.

The plan is iterative. We assess, intervene where the evidence supports it, reassess, and adjust. Most clients begin with a comprehensive baseline, then enter 3–6 months of active optimization with quarterly check-ins. Long-term care settles into an annual cadence with interval testing.

What we don't do: unregulated peptide protocols, research-only compounds, or "anti-aging" claims unsupported by evidence. The science is moving fast, and we move with it carefully, not ahead of it.

Frequently asked

Longevity, answered.

What is longevity medicine? +

Longevity medicine is a personalized, root-cause approach to extending healthspan, the years lived in good function, through quantified assessment of biological aging, targeted interventions across hormones, metabolism, cognition, and lifestyle, and longitudinal follow-up.

How is this different from a regular annual physical? +

Annual physicals screen for current disease. Longevity assessments quantify your biological age and trajectory across systems (epigenetic, cardiometabolic, neurocognitive, mitochondrial) and build a personalized plan to modify that trajectory before disease emerges.

Do you prescribe hormone replacement therapy (HRT)? +

Yes, when clinically appropriate. HRT decisions follow individual assessment of hormone metabolism, cardiovascular risk, and patient goals: with ongoing monitoring and documentation. We use bioidentical preparations and track response across multiple cycles, not just symptom self-report.

Do you offer peptide therapy? +

We discuss peptide options where the evidence base and regulatory status support their use. We do not promote unregulated or research-only compounds for general optimization. This is one of the lines we hold tightly, the longevity peptide market is a mix of legitimate clinical use and noise, and we're conservative.

How long is a longevity program? +

Most clients begin with a comprehensive assessment, followed by 3–6 months of active optimization with quarterly follow-up. Long-term care is structured around an annual cadence with interval testing in between, sustained trajectory work, not a single push.
